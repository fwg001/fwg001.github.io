  <h1>8 Takeaways for UX Designers</h1>
  <p class="intro">This year's edition of the <a href="https://www.bayareaaccessibilitycamp.com/">Bay Area Accessibility Camp</a> included a wide range of interesting topics. It was hosted at Github headquarters in San Francisco, CA.
  <br/><br/>Here are some of the main takeaways from my own notes, supplemented by AI transcriptions (Granola) and reformatted to slides by Claude.
  </p><!--more-->
